About Us

How Do We Work?

We, At SARDAR JI Building Solutions, Operates primarily in the fields of Supplying Building Materials and painting contracting with a talented team painter.

We are leading Building Material suppliers stationed at Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh doing business in CHATTISGARH, MADHYA PRADESH, MAHARASHTRA, JHARKHAND, ORISSA. We at SARDAR JI Building Solutions, provide Architects, Interior Designers, civil contractors, Painters, Plumbers, Vaastu Facilities, etc. as a part of the service. We deal with very famous and Indian Railways approved brands such as TATA, GK, JINDAL PANTHER, MSP, and others like KAMDHENU, GOEL, etc. We are distributors and suppliers of TATA PRAVESH DOORS, GK TMT, ULTRATECH CEMENT, ECOREX AAC BLOCKS, ASIAN PAINTS, INDIGO PAINTS, BIRLA WALL CARE AND WHITE CEMENT, SINTEX WATER TANKS, ICA WOOD COATINGS & LAMINATIONS, SUPREME PVC PIPES, SAND, AGGREGATE, STAINLESS STEEL DOORS & WATER TANKS & MANY MORE...

We also have the concept of Buildmart of Building Materials such as Reti (Sand), Gitti( Aggregate), TMT Bars, Cement, AAC Blocks, Bricks, Paint, Putty, Water-Tank, PVC Pipes, Wood Polish, Pest Control, Rain Water Harvesting, Waterproofing, Generator Set, ACP, Venetian Plaster, Tiles & Granite, Plywood, Sanitaryware, uPVC Windows, Electrical, Automation, GI Sheet, Power Backups & Inverter, Veneers and lots more. Also, specialize in all kinds of residential and commercial Painting work.

We are an authorized dealer of Asian Paint, Indigo Paint, Birla Wall Care, Sirca Wood Coating, ICA, Blue dip Thinner, and lots of more materials which are required for Painting of your Building, Commercial spaces, and houses. We have a large team of Painter who are expert in painting. We take With Material Labour Contract for painting.

We have set ourselves apart from other painting contractors in the market. We take pride in our craftsmanship, our quality assurance, and our track record of our ever-lasting relationships with our esteemed customers.
